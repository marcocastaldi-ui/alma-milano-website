What ALMA does

ALMA is a daily AI concierge for the Milan office — pulling real city data, writing its own script, and delivering it as a video. No human in the loop.

🗄️

Dataset creation

Every month, ALMA scrapes Milan's cultural calendar — concerts, exhibitions, theatre — and stores it in Supabase alongside daily weather from Open-Meteo.

🎙️

AI speech

Claude Sonnet reads the day's events and weather, then writes a 60–100 second briefing in ALMA's own voice — warm, local, specific. Every day, unique.

🎬

Video generation

ElevenLabs converts the script to audio, Kling AI generates the avatar video, Whisper times the subtitles, ffmpeg assembles the final branded MP4.

📬

Email delivery

Resend delivers a branded HTML email to 84 Performance Team members with a one-click CTA to watch the briefing. Done by 9 AM, every weekday.

🌐

Website & archive

alma-milano.com hosts every briefing in a live archive. Built by Claude Sonnet, deployed on Vercel, updated automatically with each new video.
